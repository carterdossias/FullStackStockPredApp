About This Full Stack App

Our Stock Price Prediction App leverages cutting-edge machine learning techniques to forecast future stock prices. By integrating historical stock data with real-time sentiment analysis from news articles, the app provides a comprehensive view of market trends.

Built with Python, Flask, TensorFlow/Keras, and various data science libraries, the platform demonstrates how AI can be seamlessly integrated into financial analysis and forecasting.

Data is automatically imported from Yahoo Finance and enriched with sentiment scores derived from news, offering an innovative approach to visualizing and predicting stock performance.
